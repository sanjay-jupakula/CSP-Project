Purification tablets/halogens, have always been a popular choice for outdoor trekkers and travellers to treat water. For a time, they were the only alternative to boiling water. Essentially they use chemicals to kill off microbiological contaminants from the water.However, you need to get the quantities of both the water and the chemical.



What does water purification tablets remove:

  • Bacteria
  • Virus
  • Protoza

What doesn’t water purification tablets remove:

  • Lead
  • Debris
  • Nitrates
  • Pesticides

Advantages of water purification tablets:

  1. Very effective for removing organisms.
  2. Cheap and cost effective
